Homeless Pets

Approximately 6.5 million pets enter US animal shelters annually. This numeber skyrockets in cases of a natural disaster, especially hurricanes, or a pandemic. Thousands of pets get left behind in emergencies.
Sadly, approximately 1.5 million shelter animals are euthanized each year.

These estimates are based in part on Shelter Animals Count data and other known and estimated sources, 2015-2018.

Our Mission

"Liberandum" is the Latin of "rescue". Our group refuses to let animals get left behind to starve. We work on rescuing pets in negelectful situations or those surrendered by their owners. We place pets in loving, caring, and funful environemnt.

Adoption

We welcome people willing to adopt a pet. We do have requirements listed in the adoption guidlines to insure your readiness to bring in a pet to the family. The policy is to insure a good and responsible relation between the owner and the pet.
