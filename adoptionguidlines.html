II.Adoption Steps:

1. Application

After you have decided which pet you would like to adopt, fill in the Application and
email it to us at: Liberandum@hotmail.com. You will be filling an in-depth survey that
includes proof that your landlord allows pets and income verification.

2.Home Visit

After receiving your application and making sure you are quilified for adoption, we
require a home visit. We will email you the day and time we are available to send a
representative of our organization to your home. You may decline the given appointment
and ask for another. Another will be send to you the next day. What we expect to see in your home is mentioned in the application form.

3.Age requirement

The applicant must be at lest 18 years old.

4.Valid Identification

The applicant must show a valid Identification card that shows his/her address and age.
The card should be present during the home visit and during the visit to the shelter.

5.Family Meet

Then comes the time to bring your family and come meet the pet. This includes other
pets already in the house. We need to make sure we are adopting our pet out to a
comfortable environment. An adult should be present with his/her ID to be admitted in.
